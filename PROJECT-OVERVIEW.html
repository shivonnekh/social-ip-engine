Project Overview

Social IP Engine

An AI system that lets our content-creator personalities reply to their social media audience automatically — instantly, on-brand, and in the right language, around the clock.

Live on Instagram & Facebook

What it does

When a follower comments or sends a private message to one of our creator accounts, the system understands it and replies in the creator's own voice — no staff member needs to be online.

1

A follower reaches out

They leave a comment on a post or send a direct message to the creator's page.

2

The system understands it

It reads the message and works out what the person is really asking about.

3

It replies as the creator

A warm, on-brand answer is sent back in seconds — publicly or as a private message.

Who it speaks as

Each creator personality has its own voice, language, and style. The system keeps every reply consistent with that identity.
